Saturn

Welcome to the Planet with visible rings, the one and only Saturn! This is a gas planet, meaning it's just a bunch of swirling gasses including hydrogen and helium above it's core. One day or rotation here is 10.7 hours, and a whole year or orbit is 29.4 Earth Years. Saturn's tilt is 26.73 degrees, meaning that it would also experience seasons like Earth. Like Jupiter, Saturn has many moons, this time at a whopping 146. Saturn's rings are composed of billions of small chunks of ice and rock coated with other materials such as dust. Interestingly though, each ring orbits at different speeds around the planet

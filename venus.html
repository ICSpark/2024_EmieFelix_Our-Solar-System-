Venus

Venus, aka Earth's evil twin. Although Venus is similar in structure and size to Earth, it has a thick atmosphere. Becasue of this, many greenhouse gasses get trapped and thus, Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system making it unable to support life. (If you havent guessed it, Venera means Venus in Russian). The longest these probes have survived was 2 hours because it was too hot on Venus.

Neptune

Welcome to Neptune! Neptune is the last planet within the solar system, and the other Gas Giant! Winds on neptune reach speeds of up to 1,200 mph and temperatures of -391 degrees farenheight. Neptune's atmosphere consists of mainly hydrogen and helium with a little bit of methane. A day on Neptune is 16 hours while a year is 165 Earth years or one Neptonian. Neptune has 16 known moons, and the most well known is Triton. Neptune has 5 main rings and 4 arcs made up of rocks and dust.
